FAQ
Why water bag is used in Marine Industry…?

Water bags are used as an alternative solid test weights for crane inspection. Water bags are can easily handle in non accessible area ,light weight, compact sizes, Sea water availability and drainage facility of sea water at yard etc. So water bags are important in Marine industry.

What is meant by Water bag…?

Water weight bag is the safe water filled test weight used for the proof load testing certification of lifting equipment’s alternative to the solid test weight.Rig owners or Ship owners utilizing our water weight hiring services or water weight rental services in order to meet the crane load test requirements during crane load testing time.

Importance of water bag in Marine Industry…?

Water bagis an alternative options for solid test weight or crane weights and safe &economical even we can use in the limited access areas for performing LOLER Inspection or Lifting Accessories Inspection.

Is the Water bag Calibrated…?

Yes.. Frequency is every 6 month

What is meant by Digital Dynamometer Load cell …

Dynamometer is a load cell or transducer which can be used as load /force measuring system either used as horizontal or vertical .When the display shows as digital in order to interpret the value is more fast/accurate;it’s called digital type dynamometer load cell.
